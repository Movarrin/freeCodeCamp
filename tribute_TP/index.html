Terry Pratchett
"May You Live In Interesting Pants!"
1948 - 2015

Sir Terry Pratchett was born in 1948 and left all too early in 2015. Sadly his passing was hastened by early onset Alzheimer's.

Sir Terry was knighted in 2009.

He was a very prolific author most well known for his Discworld series of books. First published at age thirteen he moved into a role at a newspaper. A chance comment while conducting an interview led him to publish his first book The Carpet People.

His Discworld series of books were far and away his most popular works. He lampooned religion, politics, pop culture, and classical literature and did so with a unique and touching wit.

One of his most beloved characters was Death. Somehow he made this entity someone you root for, even love. Death had many great adventures but never cruel.

Alas, eventually Death comes for us all.
